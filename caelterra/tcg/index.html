Caelterra TCG

Build decks, command factions, and battle across the world of Caelterra in a fantasy trading card game by Scrollrealm.

Collectible Strategy Across the Realms

Caelterra TCG is built around faction identity, resource tension, and tactical choices. Players assemble decks around leaders, relics, units, spells, and realm-shaping events.

  • Build around one or more faction identities.
  • Win through board control, tempo, attrition, or objective pressure.
  • Expand through starter sets, booster waves, and organized play support.

Designed for Discoverability

Card previews, faction guides, starter deck pages, and product pages are separated so that players can search for exactly what they need.
